International Technological University

ITU was established as a non-profit institution with the sole purpose of giving students a top-notch education that was closely related to current business trends. Today,It is uphold this principle by embracing a diverse group of students at ITU who share the same goal of advancing their education and achieving professional success.
ITU's goal is to support students who are especially interested in high-tech entrepreneurship with excellent education. All programs have an applied bent, with an emphasis on speciality areas adapted to the market demands of Silicon Valley enterprises.
ITUs Computer Science Department focuses on the software engineering and computer science disciplines. Both programs in the department place primary emphasis on the technical aspects of building and modifying high quality software systems.
